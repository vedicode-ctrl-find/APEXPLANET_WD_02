About This Project

SwaadRachnay is more than a digital cookbook — it’s a handcrafted space where simplicity meets flavor. Whether you’re exploring Indian classics or crafting your own creations, this app is your personal kitchen diary.

Built during my web development journey, this project reflects my passion for both code and cuisine. You can add, edit, and explore recipes, organize your cooking through a smart to-do list, and even plan your meals — all saved right in your browser, no login needed.

This isn't just a website — it’s a growing archive of meals, memories, and the joy of home-cooked food.

Thank you for being part of this culinary adventure. Let’s keep cooking!
